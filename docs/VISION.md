# Future You — Vision

*The product vision and the end-to-end experience. Pairs with [`../RE-ENVISION.md`](../RE-ENVISION.md) (decisions + phased plan), [`DESIGN.md`](DESIGN.md) (look & feel + references), [`METHODOLOGY.md`](METHODOLOGY.md) (the finance), and [`DATA.md`](DATA.md) (data & AI). Written 2026-06-29.*

## What it is
Future You is a personal investing **research & education assistant** for everyday people (ages ~15+). It builds you an optimized, personalized portfolio — **and makes you understand and believe it.** The plan is the output; *confidence and comprehension are the point.*

## The problem & the bet
Most tools hand you an allocation and a pie chart. If you don't understand it or don't trust it, you either never act, or you bail at the first dip. **An allocation you don't believe in is useless.** The bet: if we *teach as we recommend* — explaining every choice in plain language, balancing what *you* want with what's optimal, and letting you see the reasoning (and the maths) whenever you want — you become a calm, informed, durable investor.

## Principles (non-negotiable)
1. **Research & education first** — every recommendation is explained; nothing is a black box.
2. **Recommend *with* you, not at you** — you can want any holding (even "risky" ones); we include it at a responsible, risk-adjusted weight and explain the trade-off.
3. **Accuracy is sacred** — real data only; AI never invents a number; every figure is traceable and cited (DATA.md).
4. **Explain for a 15-year-old — without dumbing down** — simple language, optional depth ("show the maths"), tap-to-define jargon.
5. **Adaptive to you** — surface only the planning scenarios that actually apply.
6. **Secure & accountless** — no logins, no stored personal data (RE-ENVISION §4).
7. **Honest, not addictive** — encouragement and celebration, never dark patterns (DESIGN §1, §9).
8. **Built for Switzerland, in EN & FR** — a UCITS-first, tax-/fee-smart plan for a Swiss resident ([`SWITZERLAND.md`](SWITZERLAND.md)); fully bilingual (English / French).

## Who it's for
Curious-but-not-confident individuals ~15+: a first-time saver, a parent planning ahead, a student learning the ropes. No finance background assumed; no condescension given. Based in **Switzerland** (built for a Geneva family), so the app is **Swiss-optimized** and **bilingual — English and French**.

## The experience — end to end
A guided, mostly one-thing-at-a-time journey (DESIGN §6), with deep research and analytics on demand. The path **adapts** to who you are.

1. **Land & start** — the public landing (DESIGN §12) states the promise in one screen → "Start".
2. **About you** — a short, friendly profile: age, who depends on you, how steady your income is, your cushion, any debts, what you care about. This quietly drives your *ability* to take risk and which scenarios apply.
3. **Your goal(s)** — pick or define goals (retirement, a home, education, a safety net, a dream purchase, building wealth…). The app **auto-detects** which planning modules apply — you never bolt one on by hand.
4. **How much risk suits you** — a short quiz (one question per screen) measures *willingness*; combined with *ability* from your profile, we land on a risk profile and **explain why** in plain words.
5. **What you're into** — optionally name sectors or companies you like (including "risky" ones). This is where "recommend *with* you" begins.
6. **Your plan** — the personalized allocation: a diversified core **plus your picks at responsibly adjusted weights**, a plain-language "why" for every holding, the mix shown as a donut/treemap (DESIGN §12).
7. **Your growth** — a projection cone (a *range* of futures, not one fake line) and your goal-funding probability.
8. **How risky is it** — the risk picture in plain language ("a bad year could look like…"), with a "grown-up detail" toggle for the full analytics.
9. **Research, in depth** — open any holding or sector for a full dashboard: what it is, the industry, key numbers in plain words, why it's safe/risky, bull vs bear, analyst views (sourced), and its role in *your* plan (the NVIDIA flow, below).
10. **Your situation, handled** — adaptive modules appear as relevant: retirement income, education saving, home deposit, emergency fund, debt-vs-invest.
11. **How to actually invest (Switzerland)** — the practical guide: pillar 3a, 35% withholding & reclaim, stamp duty, no capital-gains tax for private investors, how to open an account. *(full spec: [`SWITZERLAND.md`](SWITZERLAND.md))*
12. **Summary & take it with you** — a one-page plan / IPS to download (PDF) and a shareable read-only link. No account needed.
13. **Learn, anytime** — a Learn hub + glossary; every jargon word anywhere is tappable for a plain definition (DESIGN §5).

## The canonical flow — "I want NVIDIA"
The experience that defines the product:
1. You add **NVIDIA** to what you're interested in.
2. It's flagged higher-risk → the app **explains why** (rich valuation, high beta, concentration, the "AI-bubble" thesis) and **teaches what an "AI bubble" even is.**
3. A **deep research dashboard** opens: the business, the industry, the numbers in plain words, bull vs bear, analyst targets (sourced + dated), the risks.
4. The plan **still allocates to NVIDIA — sized down** to a responsible level, and **explains the sizing**: *"you wanted this; here's a measured amount that fits your risk without betting your plan on it."*

You stay in control, better informed, with a plan you believe in. (The finance behind this: METHODOLOGY §5 — investor views/tilts + risk budgeting.)

## The teaching layer (everywhere)
- Every formula has an optional, genuinely-explained breakdown: a cleanly typeset equation, each symbol defined, a worked example with *your* numbers, an analogy (DESIGN §5).
- Tap any term → a plain-language definition; a dedicated glossary/Learn page anchors it.
- Tone: simple enough for 15, never tedious, never condescending.

## What success looks like
- A user can **explain their own plan** in a sentence and feels calm about it.
- They understand *why* each holding is there, and *why* their risky pick is sized the way it is.
- It's **live at one URL**, works on a phone, and a recruiter can open a demo and immediately get it.
- The finance underneath is interview-grade and defensible (METHODOLOGY), and every number is real and verified (DATA).

## Non-goals & scope guardrails
- **Educational, not personalized financial advice.** FinSA-aware framing and clear disclaimers. Not a brokerage — we never execute trades or move money.
- **Accountless** — no stored personal financial data.
- **No dark patterns** — no streak-guilt, no pressure to act, no manufactured urgency.
- Launch scope is an **individual** tool; multi-user/family profiles are a possible later addition, not v1.
